Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Please review our return policy below.

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days of receiving your order for a full refund. The item must be unused, in its original packaging, and in the same condition as when you received it.

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Contact our customer support team at support@kilnenterprise.com to request a return authorization.
  2. Pack the item securely in its original packaging.
  3. Include the return authorization form provided by our team.
  4. Ship the item back to us using a trackable shipping method.

Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Personalized or custom-made items.
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" on the product page.
  • Gift cards and downloadable software products.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately at support@kilnenterprise.com. We will provide instructions for returning the item and will send a replacement at no additional cost.
